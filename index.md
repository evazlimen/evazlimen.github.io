Hi! I am Eva Zlimen, a second year planetary science PhD student at the University of California, Los Angeles. I completed my undergrad at UC Santa Cruz (Go Slugs!). At UCLA, I work with Prof. Hilke Schlichting as part of <a href="https://faculty.epss.ucla.edu/~hilke/Hilke/Welcome.html"> PEARL</a>, where I am broadly interested in creating new models to understand planet formation and evolution. More specifically, I am interested in interior-atmosphere interactions and the thermal evolution of planets following giant impacts.
