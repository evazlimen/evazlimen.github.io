Hi! I am Eva Zlimen, a second year planetary science PhD student at the University of California, Los Angeles. I completed my undergrad at UC Santa Cruz (Go Slugs!). At UCLA, I work with Prof. Hilke Schlichting as part of the <a href="https://faculty.epss.ucla.edu/~hilke/Hilke/Welcome.html"> PEARL</a> lab, where I am broadly interested in understanding planet formation and evolution. More specifically, I am interested in interior-atmosphere interactions and the thermal evolution of planets following giant impacts. Check out the <a href="/research"> Research</a> tab to learn more about what I'm up to!<br>
I gratefully acknowledge research support from the NSF GRFP.
